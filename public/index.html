Under the Aegis of ICAS

Every certificate is issued by the Council, not printed locally.

The Indian Council of Astrological Sciences has trained astrologers since 1984, headquartered in Chennai. Our chapter — ICAS, based at Purushottamanand Ashram — teaches to that same national syllabus and certification standard.

Common Questions

Before you register

Do I need any background in astrology to start?

No — Jyotish Praveena assumes no prior knowledge and starts from the fundamentals.

Is the certificate valid outside Lucknow?

Yes. It's issued by the national ICAS office in Chennai, not by this chapter locally, so it carries the same standing wherever you go.

Can I switch between online and offline mid-course?

Speak to us directly about this — batches are generally run in one mode, but we try to accommodate genuine circumstances.

What happens after I register?

You'll create a student account, upload your documents, and pay the course fee — once approved, you're allocated to a batch and notified by email.
