Policy

Privacy Policy

We collect the personal information you provide during registration and admission — including name, contact details, address, and identity/education documents — solely to process admissions, deliver course content, and communicate about your enrolment.

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Uploaded documents are stored securely and access is restricted to authorised chapter administrators reviewing your admission.

You may request a copy of the data we hold about you, or request its deletion, by contacting us through the details on our Contact page — subject to record-keeping requirements for issued certificates.

Policy

Terms & Conditions

Enrolment in any course is confirmed only after admission approval and receipt of full or instalment payment as specified for that course. Course access, live class links, and study material are provided solely to the enrolled student and may not be shared or redistributed.

Certification is issued only on satisfactory completion of the course's assessment requirements, as set by ICAS under the standards of the Indian Council of Astrological Sciences.

We reserve the right to reschedule live sessions or batches with reasonable notice, and to update course content to keep it current with our syllabus.

Policy

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Requests for cancellation and refund made before a batch's start date are eligible for a full refund, less any payment gateway charges already incurred. Once a batch has started, refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed.

Instalment payments already made toward a course are non-transferable to another course unless approved by chapter administration.

To request a refund, contact us with your registered email and payment receipt number via the Contact page.
