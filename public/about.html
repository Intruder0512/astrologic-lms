ICAS AstroLogic Chapter operates under the aegis of the Indian Council of Astrological Sciences — training students in classical Jyotish, Vastu and Nakshatra work since the Council's founding in 1984.

Our Method

The chart is a syllabus. We teach it like one.

A kundli divides a life into twelve houses, each preparing the reading of the next. Our courses are structured the same way — Jyotish Praveena lays the foundation, Jyotish Visharada builds analytical depth, and Jyotish Bhushan turns study into research practice. Vastu and Nakshatra tracks run in parallel for students who want to specialise.

Every instructor at the chapter consults professionally outside the classroom. What's taught in a module is drawn from real charts, not just textbook cases.

What Governs Us

Affiliation, Not Franchise

This chapter follows the syllabus, certification standards and code of conduct set by the Indian Council of Astrological Sciences (Regd.), Chennai — the same body that has certified astrologers nationally since 1984.
