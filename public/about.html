A small chapter, teaching one thing properly.

ICAS is a registered chapter of the Indian Council of Astrological Sciences (founded 1984, headquartered in Chennai), based at Purushottamanand Ashram in Hasanganj, Lucknow. We teach two courses — Jyotish Praveena and Jyotish Visharada — both properly, rather than many things loosely.

Our Method

The chart is a syllabus. We teach it like one.

A kundli divides a life into twelve houses, each preparing the reading of the next. Our two courses follow the same logic: Jyotish Praveena lays the foundation — the zodiac, the planets, the houses, how to actually read a chart. Jyotish Visharada builds on it — dasha systems, divisional charts, and structured prediction.

Both are taught by Shri Markandeya Shukla, our centre coordinator, either online over live sessions or in person at the Ashram — the syllabus and certification are identical either way.

Your Instructor

Shri Markandeya Shukla

Shri Markandeya Shukla serves as Centre Coordinator for ICAS at Purushottamanand Ashram, teaching both Jyotish Praveena and Jyotish Visharada directly to every batch — online and in person. Students work with the same instructor from their first class through certification, rather than being handed between rotating faculty.

Classes run in both English and Hindi, with course material and examinations available in either medium depending on what a batch needs.
